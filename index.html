About

From hands-on engineering to program leadership

Engineering Project Manager at NBCUniversal with 10 years of enterprise delivery experience, bridging program execution and applied AI. I manage complex, high-stakes programs across infrastructure, cloud, and security -- bringing structure to ambiguity and turning technical complexity into business outcomes.

I started my career writing code, migrating databases, and troubleshooting infrastructure. That technical foundation is what makes me different as a program manager. I don't just manage timelines -- I understand the systems, the trade-offs, and the technical decisions behind them.

Over the past decade, I've worked across the full spectrum of enterprise technology: cloud infrastructure at NBCUniversal, process automation consulting at DocuPhase, and custom solution delivery at Fairfax Imaging. Today, I focus on leading large-scale technology programs, implementing AI-driven solutions, and helping organizations turn technical complexity into competitive advantage.
